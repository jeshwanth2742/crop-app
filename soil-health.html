Soil Health & Fertilizer Guide

Loamy Soil

Good for wheat and maize. Fertilizer: Nitrogen 20kg/acre, Phosphorus 10kg/acre.

Clayey Soil

Suitable for rice. Fertilizer: Nitrogen 25kg/acre, Potash 15kg/acre. Ensure proper drainage.
